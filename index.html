⚠ Trust Erosion

The Settlement Black Hole

The exact moment a customer is ready for their next product is when we ignore them.

"سددت أول بأول لمدة 24 شهر وخلصت.. لي 5 أشهر أتواصل معهم أقولهم خلصت، ليه ما سويتوا مخالصة؟"

The Friction

Loyal completers trapped in a post-settlement void. No digital closure, frozen accounts, and SIMAH delays turn advocates into detractors.

⚠ Transparency Gap

The Clarity Breakdown

Uninformed customers feel deceived. Clarity is the foundation of trust.

02
Voice of Customer

"لي تسع شهور ملاحظة مبلغ القسط يرتفع.. طلع إن كل ما أستخدم رصيد بسيطة يصير لي أقساط زيادة.. موظف بسيطة ما وضح لي."

The Problem

CRM sends no transparency signal. Customers discover financial impacts months later. The onboarding fails to explain revolving credit mechanics.

The CRM Fix
2

Real-Time Transparency: Every utilization triggers a push: "Your new installment will be X." Immediate confidence.

"Clarity turns a transaction into a relationship."
⚠ SAMA Reputation Risk

Reputation vs. Collections

Aggressive collections destroy brand equity and increase regulatory exposure.

03
Voice of Customer

"صار لي ظرف تأخرت صار جوالي كله رسائل تهديد من موظفينهم.. نصيحة لا تأخذون منهم."

The Failure

The system treats all customers equally. A first-time delay from a 5-year loyal customer receives the same "threat" as a chronic defaulter.

The Solution
3

Loyalty-Informed Collections: Empathy-first messaging for high-tier customers. CRM segments collections behavior by LTV.

"The message that loses a loyal customer is the most expensive message Tasheel sends."
