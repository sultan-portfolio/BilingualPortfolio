The Goal

The Problem

Customers who complete their obligation get trapped in a post-settlement void — bank accounts frozen, no digital closure confirmation, no SIMAH update.

A potential automation gap between collections, SIMAH reporting, and closure confirmation turns loyal completers into angry detractors.

The CRM Fix
1
Instant Closure JourneyFinal payment triggers: digital settlement certificate + automated SIMAH update within 24 hours.
2
Re-engagement OfferCRM fires personalized next-product offer 7 days post-closure. Settlement becomes a sales trigger.
KPI: Settlement-to-offer conversion rate
"The customer who finishes paying is your best prospect for the next product. The settlement moment is a loyalty event — not a closing file."

The Problem

The CRM knows every transaction but sends no transparency signal. Customers discover the financial impact only after months of accumulation.

The onboarding journey fails to explain revolving credit mechanics — leaving customers to discover the rules after the damage is done.

The CRM Fix
1
Real-Time NotificationsEvery credit utilization triggers instant push: "Your new installment will be X for Y months." Within 60 seconds.
Basita App Feature

Interactive dashboard showing how each withdrawal affects the monthly payment — before the customer confirms.

KPI: Billing complaint recurrence rate
"Clarity is the foundation of loyalty. A customer who understands what they owe stays. A customer who discovers it later leaves — and tells ten people."

The Problem

The collections system treats all customers equally — a first-time delay from a loyal customer gets the same aggressive response as a chronic defaulter.

Root cause: no customer segmentation in CRM. Without loyalty tiers informing collections behavior, years of trust are destroyed in one SMS. This also carries SAMA compliance risk under consumer protection guidelines.

The CRM Fix
1
Loyalty-Informed CollectionsHigh-tier customers receive support-first messaging on first delay. Escalation follows tier-appropriate grace window.
2
Supportive Collections ScriptReplace threat messages with: "We noticed a delay — can we help?" First contact is empathy, not enforcement.
KPI: Loyal customer churn rate post-delay
"Retaining a loyal customer costs 5x less than acquiring a new one. The collections script that loses a loyal customer is the most expensive message Tasheel ever sends — to the customer, and to the regulator."
