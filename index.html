The Vision

The Opportunity Hidden in Plain Sight

This is not a critique of current operations. It is a strategic map of untapped loyalty potential discovered within the existing customer journey.

1. The Diagnostic Approach

I analyzed Tasheel’s external feedback ecosystem and identified systemic patterns. I looked specifically for moments where customers explicitly wanted to remain loyal, but were blocked by operational friction or communication gaps.

2. The Governance Lens

CRM is not just a tool; it is a Governance Engine. Every automated SMS, push notification, and collection call must be carefully measured by how much it either adds to, or subtracts from, the overall Customer Lifetime Value (LTV).

3. The eXtra Closed-Loop

We possess an unfair advantage in the market. By leveraging the retail relationship, we can create a "closed-loop" ecosystem where accessible finance fuels retail shopping, and retail shopping loyalty fuels financial compliance.

4. The Ultimate Goal

Transitioning Tasheel from a model of reactive customer support to a standard of Proactive Loyalty Management. This means anticipating friction before it occurs through intelligent, data-driven touchpoints.

The Root Problem

Customers who successfully complete their financial obligations are entering a post-settlement void. Instead of being rewarded for their commitment, their bank accounts remain frozen and SIMAH profiles go un-updated due to operational lag. This turns your absolute best, most reliable prospects into active detractors of the brand.

The CRM Fix
1
Instant Closure API

Auto-trigger the generation of a digital settlement certificate and push the SIMAH clearance update within exactly 24 hours of the final payment clearing.

2
Automated Re-engagement

Deploy a sequenced "Thank You" campaign that celebrates their commitment, immediately followed by offering a pre-approved limit for their next eXtra purchase.

"The customer who finishes paying is not the end of a lifecycle; they are your warmest, most qualified lead. Treat the settlement as a celebration."
Pattern 02
⚠ Clarity Gap

Basita Transparency

02
Customer Voice

"طلع إن كل ما استخدم رصيد بسيطة المتاح لي يصير لي أقساط زيادة وتتراكم.. موظف بسيطة ما وضح لي أبداً والتطبيق ما يعطيني المجموع."

App Store User Review
The Root Problem

A severe lack of real-time financial transparency is causing "Billing Shock." The CRM and backend systems know exactly what the new cumulative installment amount will be, but the customer only discovers it months later when the deduction hits. This destroys trust and leads to immediate app deletion.

The CRM Fix
1
Predictive Impact Dashboard

Implement an in-app visual calculator that previews the exact new monthly total before the customer hits confirm on utilizing their available credit.

2
Real-time Ledger Signals

Trigger an instant, unavoidable CRM push notification upon usage: "Success. Please note your new consolidated monthly plan is now X for the next Y months."

The Root Problem

Aggressive, standardized collection scripts are currently being applied to all customer segments equally. A highly profitable, loyal 2-year customer experiencing a first-time delay is being spoken to with the exact same tone as a high-risk chronic defaulter, permanently destroying brand equity.

The CRM Fix
★
Behavioral Segmentation

Configure the CRM to identify high-tier loyalists and actively route them to a specialized "Support-First" message sequence rather than a standard demand.

✔
Automated Brand Guardrails

Implement strict tone-control parameters for outbound SMS and dialer scripts based dynamically on the customer's tenure and historical payment score.

The Competitive Advantage

The eXtra Moat.

Most financial institutions have to pay heavily to acquire a customer. Tasheel is uniquely positioned to dominate the market by fully integrating the CRM with the eXtra retail footprint.

Zero CAC Acquisition

Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) is the biggest drain on profitability. By leveraging eXtra's massive daily footfall, we can feed Tasheel's CRM with high-intent data before the financing conversation even begins. We know what they want to buy before they know how they will pay for it.

The Retention Flywheel

Loyalty points earned through on-time Tasheel repayments should be designed for redemption exclusively back at eXtra. This creates a psychological and financial "lock-in" effect—a continuous flywheel where financing guarantees retail sales, and retail rewards guarantee financing compliance.
