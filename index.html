Pattern 01: The Settlement Void

Closing the Loop on Trust.

Systemic Friction
TRUST EROSION

"سددت أول بأول لمدة 24 شهر وخلصت.. لي 5 أشهر أتواصل معهم أقولهم خلصت، ليه ما سويتوا مخالصة؟"

The CRM Gap:

A "Post-Settlement Void" where high-quality customers are ignored after their obligation is met. No automated confirmation means no next-product trigger.

The Loyalty Solution:

  • 1. Automated Digital Closure: Immediate in-app 'Success' certificate.
  • 2. Next-Best-Offer (NBO): Triggering the next financing eligibility 7 days post-settlement.
  • 3. SIMAH Visibility: In-app status tracker to reduce call center load.
Pattern 02: Informed Collections

Loyalty-Based Risk Management.

Operational Conflict
BRAND EQUITY RISK

"عميلكم من سنوات، صار لي ظرف تأخرت أسبوع واحد، صار جوالي كله رسائل تهديد.."

The CRM Gap:

Treating all customers equally in distress. Aggressive collections scripts for loyal, first-time-late customers destroy LTV in a single call.

The Loyalty Solution:

  • 1. Tiered Outreach: 'Support-First' tone for high-tier loyalists.
  • 2. Grace Windows: Automatic 48-hour window before penalties for 'Gold' members.
  • 3. SAMA Alignment: Ensuring fair-treatment principles are baked into the CRM workflow.
